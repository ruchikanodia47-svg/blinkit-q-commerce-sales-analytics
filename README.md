# ⚡ Blinkit Quick-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Business Intelligence workspace built in Power BI to analyze high-velocity sales trends, customer payment mechanics, delivery logistics bottlenecks, and localized profitability for Blinkit operations over a full fiscal year[cite: 3].

This project demonstrates complex multi-dimensional dashboard design, advanced operational metric tracking, and product line decomposition using Power BI.

### 💡 Data Insights & Operational Metrics
* **Sales Volume Stability Over Time:** Monthly customer demand remains highly resilient, consistently fluctuating in a narrow channel between **$0.11M** and **$0.14M** throughout the calendar year[cite: 3].
* **Category Revenue Leaders vs. Delivery Efficiency:** 
  * **Staples** represents the largest grossing category generating **$236K** in sales with a highly optimized delivery speed of 18.49 minutes[cite: 3].
  * **Fruits & Vegetables** ($212K sales) clocks the platform's fastest dispatch and delivery metric at a blazing **18.25 minutes**[cite: 3].
  * **Personal Care** items take the longest logistically to fulfill, averaging a peak wait time of **19.59 minutes**[cite: 3].
* **Transactional Settlement Preferences:** Users rely on diverse modern fintech tools to execute rapid checking out. **Digital Wallets** capture the plurality of transactions at **21.65% ($310K)**, followed evenly by Debit Cards (20.0%), Cash on Delivery (19.75%), Credit Cards (~19.0%), and UPI (18.83%)[cite: 3].
* **Regional Margin Decomposition:** Deep-dive analysis via a decomposition tree isolates operational margin leakage across major city centers, with **Kolkata (-$166,495)** and **Hyderabad (-$163,921)** drawing the largest cost imbalances[cite: 3].
* **Category Profit Leaks:** Waterfall tracking maps categorical overhead accurately, revealing that **Staples (-$0.19M)** and **Frozen Foods (-$0.18M)** present the highest bottom-line margin constraints on the platform[cite: 3].

### 🛠️ Technical Implementation Details
* **Waterfall & Tree Diagnostics:** Deployed advanced visual breakdown mechanics (Decomposition Trees and Waterfall Charts) to isolate row-level margin drops by geography and catalog classification[cite: 3].
* **Logistics Dual-Axis Modeling:** Combined volume bar trends alongside line chart representations to evaluate if item sales volume correlates directly with fulfillment delay rates[cite: 3].
* **Global Dynamic Slicers:** Integrated top-level control nodes allowing full workspace filtering based on precise *Order Date ranges*, *Product Category*, and *City selection*[cite: 3].
* **Color Psychology Customization:** Crafted a sleek dark/light dynamic layout styled intentionally around the high-contrast yellow corporate branding of quick-commerce platforms[cite: 3].
